Privacy Policy for ProLineups App

Welcome to ProLineups! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share data when you use our app. By using ProLineups, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use ProLineups, we may collect certain data to improve your experience. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as which features you use and how often.
  • Device Information: Details about your device, including model, operating system, and unique identifiers.
  • Crash Reports: Data related to app errors or crashes, collected via Firebase Crashlytics, to help us maintain a stable and reliable app.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your name, address, or payment details unless explicitly stated.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to identify popular features.
  • Debug and fix technical issues.
  • Ensure the app operates smoothly across different devices and platforms.

All collected data is anonymized where possible, and we do not use it to identify individual users.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third-party companies or advertisers. However, the data we collect is shared with trusted Google services, including:

  • Firebase Analytics: For usage tracking and performance analysis.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For identifying and fixing technical issues.

Google processes this data in accordance with its Privacy Policy. These services help us better understand how users interact with ProLineups, ensuring continuous improvements.

4. Security of Your Data

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it. This includes encryption and restricted access to the collected data. Only authorized personnel can access the data, and it is used solely for app development and improvement purposes.

While we strive to protect your data, please note that no system is entirely secure. However, we are committed to resolving any security issues promptly.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about the data we collect and how it is used.
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data, where applicable and legally permissible.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of data collection for analytics purposes by adjusting your device settings or contacting us.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please reach out to us at valorant.lineups.plg@gmail.com.

6. Use of Third-Party Services

ProLineups uses trusted third-party services, primarily from Google, to collect and process data. These services operate in compliance with global privacy standards and include:

  • Firebase Analytics: For user behavior analysis and app performance.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For diagnosing and fixing technical issues.

We encourage you to review the Google Privacy Policy to understand how your data is processed by these services.

7. Children’s Privacy

ProLineups is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
